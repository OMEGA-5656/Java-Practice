Types of Inheritance

1)Single-Level Inheritance
    Inheriting thingies from only 1 parent class.

2)Multi-Level Inheritance
    Inherits thingies from a parent class which inturn has inherited from a/many grandparent class(es).

3)Multiple Inheritance
    Inheriting from multiple parents (not possible in java,(hmm well kind of possible using interfaces)).

4)Hierarchical Inheritance
    Inhertance in a family tree like manner.

5)Hybrid Inheritance
    Combination of two or more types of inheritance.
